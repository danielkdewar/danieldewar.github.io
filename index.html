Daniel Dewar — Founder. Sound designer. Writer.

About

After working as a freelance record and mix engineer in Brisbane, Daniel moved to New York City where he worked as a sound designer in the film & television industry. While in New York, he transitioned to digital strategy and data roles before moving back to Australia in 2013.

After working in digital production, Daniel headed up the marketing & strategy team at one of Australia’s leading data and analytics agencies, leading the agency’s transformation from a consultancy to a marketing & ad technology company.

In 2016, Daniel founded Paperchain, an online marketplace for media financing.
